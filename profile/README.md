## 🌍 Our Mission

> **74% of young Australians aged 18–30 view sun damage as a status symbol rather than a health risk.**

Viral tanning trends on TikTok and Instagram are reversing decades of sun-safety progress. Traditional public health messaging can't compete with highly visual, algorithm-driven content — so we built something that speaks Gen Z's language.

**UVSense** turns abstract UV data into personal, actionable protection — making sun safety feel like a social norm, not a medical chore.
